longBiography_MD: >-
  A resident of Norwalk, Connecticut, [John F. Davenport is a financial
  advisor](https://www.behance.net/johnfdavenport) with decades of experience.
  In 1997, John F. Davenport founded Davenport & Associates, a family wealth
  planning firm that assists clients in maximizing income-producing asses while
  optimizing the transfer of wealth to heirs.


  Before founding Davenport & Associates, where he is president, John F. Davenport worked as an executive Wealth Planning Vice President at the New York based-Merrill Lynch. Additionally, he previously worked as a vice president of pension communications at MONY Financial Services, where he handled and wrote corporate communications on pension legislation for participants.


  [Mr. Davenport](https://johnfdavenport.net/) holds a BA in philosophy from the University of Notre Dame, an MBA in finance from Fordham University, and a JD from Pace University. He is a member of various organizations such as the New York Bar Association, the Connecticut Bar Association, and the Life Office Management Association.


  While not working or holding financial webinars, Mr. Davenport enjoys traveling and boxing. He also enjoys playing golf and tennis.
